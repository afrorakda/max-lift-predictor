# #61 Max Lift Predictor | Fridge Combo

A high-precision 1RM (One Rep Max) calculator built for serious trainees. No more manual math between sets—get your estimated limits and training percentages instantly with a single tap.

## 🟢 One Action, Zero Friction
- **Instant Calculation:** Results update in real-time as you adjust weight or reps. No "Calculate" button needed.
- **Smart Stepper:** Logic-linked increment system. 2.5kg steps for KG mode, 5.0lbs steps for LBS mode—matching standard gym plate increments.
- **Practical Rounding:** Results are automatically rounded to 0.5 for KG and whole integers for LBS, providing actionable numbers you can actually load onto a barbell.
- **Full Percentage Table:** Instantly see weights for 95% down to 50% of your 1RM to plan your training blocks accurately.

## 🛠 Target Users
- **Powerlifters & Bodybuilders:** To estimate 1RM without the risk of actual max-effort failure.
- **Strength Athletes:** To calculate specific training intensities (e.g., "today's work is 80% of 1RM").
- **Personal Trainers:** To quickly demonstrate strength progress to clients on the gym floor.

1. Open the tool in your mobile browser.
